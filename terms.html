03 Refund Policy

Our Position on Refunds

All ticket sales are final. We do not offer refunds except in exceptional circumstances as set out below. Please ensure you are able to attend before completing your purchase.

We understand that circumstances can change. Our refund policy is as follows:

  • Refunds will not be issued for change of mind, scheduling conflicts, or failure to attend on the day.
  • Refunds will not be issued where the purchaser is no longer able to attend due to personal circumstances that do not meet the exceptional circumstances criteria below.
  • Refunds will not be issued within 14 days of the event date under any circumstances, except where we have cancelled the event entirely.

Exceptional Circumstances

We may, at our sole discretion, consider a refund request in genuinely exceptional circumstances. These include serious illness or hospitalisation of the ticket holder or an immediate family member where medical evidence is provided, bereavement of an immediate family member occurring within 14 days of the event, or circumstances entirely beyond your control that make attendance physically impossible. Each request will be considered on its individual merits. Submitting a request does not guarantee a refund will be approved.

To request a refund under exceptional circumstances, contact us at the email address listed in Section 9 with your order reference and a brief explanation of your circumstances. We will respond within five working days.

Where a refund is approved, it will be returned to the original payment method within 10 working days. Processing fees charged by the payment provider are non-refundable.
