The Day

Everything We Hoped it Would Be

Doors opened at 12:00 and the room filled with people who had come to learn, connect, and be honest about what social commerce actually looks like from the inside. That energy never left.

Brands sat on stage alongside creators and talked straight. TikTok sent Giulia Yang to give an unfiltered platform briefing. Kelly Jones stood up and told the room she had gone from zero to £400,000 on Whatnot since November 2025. Leighton Denny MBE brought a perspective that nobody else in the UK could have given. The room paid attention.

At Novaro's studios, attendees rotated through in small batches and watched live affiliate broadcasts for Remington and George Foreman running in real time. Seeing it from the inside changed how a lot of people thought about live commerce.

The formal programme wrapped at 18:00. The bar stayed open until 20:00. The conversations kept going long after that.
