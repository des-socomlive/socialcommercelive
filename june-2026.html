The Day

Doors opened at 12:00. The formal programme wrapped at 18:00. The bar stayed open until 20:00.

12:00

Doors Open

Registration, networking, and early bar access. Bar open from 12:00 through to 20:00.

Intro and Brand Panel

10-minute welcome from Des Hamilton and Chris Grimes, followed by a 45 to 60 minute brand panel. Brands shared why social commerce is their biggest channel opportunity in 2026 and what they need from creators. Panel included Remington, Nature Spell, and Richer Sounds.

Cross-Platform Strategy Keynote

Chris Grimes on why the smartest creators and brands are moving beyond single-platform dependency. Real buyer performance data across TikTok, Instagram, Meta, and YouTube Shorts.

Live Studio Visits at Novaro

Attendees rotated in small batches to Novaro's studios in Manchester to watch live TikTok Shop broadcasts in action for Remington and George Foreman. A real affiliate live show from the inside. What drives conversions, how the show runs, and what separates good content from great.

Live Affiliate Panel

Some of the UK's most experienced live commerce creators and affiliates on stage. Real conversations about building a sustainable income from social commerce. TikTok Shop and beyond. Audience questions welcome.

Closing Keynote — Des Hamilton

Des closed the formal programme with a 30-minute keynote drawing together the themes of the day and setting out where social commerce is heading and what every creator in the room should do next.
