What is Social Commerce Live

Built by creators, for creators

This is not a conference. It is a gathering. The kind of day where the conversations in the bar are as valuable as what happens on stage, because the people in the room are the same people doing the work every day.

Social commerce is the biggest opportunity in the creator economy right now and it is still early. This day exists to help you understand it, navigate it, and build something real from it.

The Last Event

Manchester Hall. A full day from doors opening at midday through to close at 8pm. Brands on stage. TikTok in the room. Live studio visits. Real numbers shared in public. A room full of people who were genuinely glad they came.

Brands represented included Remington, Nature Spell, and Richer Sounds. Giulia Yang from TikTok gave an unfiltered platform briefing. Kelly Jones took the stage and explained how she went from zero to £400,000 on Whatnot since November 2025.
